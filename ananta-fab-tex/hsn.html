HSN classification of our fabric

The table below lists the HSN codes covering the fabric categories we manufacture and supply, for your purchase order and GST reference.

HSN CodeDescription
5212Other woven fabrics of cotton
5407Woven fabrics of synthetic filament yarn, including woven fabrics obtained from materials of heading 5404
5512Woven fabrics of synthetic staple fibres, containing 85% or more by weight of synthetic staple fibres
5208Woven fabrics of cotton, containing 85% or more by weight of cotton, weighing not more than 200 g/m²

HSN codes are provided for reference during procurement and billing. Please confirm the applicable code for your specific order at the time of purchase, as classification can vary by fabric composition and finish.
