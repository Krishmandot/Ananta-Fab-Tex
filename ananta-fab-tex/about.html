Our Story

A manufacturer built on consistency

Ananta Fab Tex is a leading fabric manufacturer based in Balotra, Barmer — one of Rajasthan's historic textile hubs. Under the proprietorship of Kavita Mandot, the firm has spent six years supplying pocketing fabric, home furnishing fabric and wedding tent fabric to buyers across India.

Every order runs through our own facility, which means tighter quality control, consistent widths and weights, and pricing without a middleman's markup. Alongside weaving, our Print Rotto service lets buyers get fabric printed to their own design under one roof.

We're committed to quality products and dependable service — the reason repeat buyers keep coming back to Ananta Fab Tex.
